# VAELT — Sitemap

## Pages

### 1. Home — `/`
**Job:** Convert cold traffic into a booked audit call.
**Sections (top to bottom):**
- Nav (fixed)
- Hero
- About (short version)
- Services (problem→solution teaser, 5-6 cards)
- How We Work (3-step band)
- How We Build (4-phase tab panel)
- Build Rules (marquee + numbered list)
- Selected Work (demo portfolio, 5 rows)
- FAQ
- Footer

**Primary CTA:** "Book a free audit" → `/contact`
**Secondary CTA:** "See all services" → `/services`

### 2. Services — `/services`
**Job:** Let a visitor self-identify their problem and see VAELT's framing of the fix, with zero prices shown.
**Sections:**
- Nav
- Page intro (short headline: "What's actually broken?")
- Full problem→solution list (expanded version of Home's teaser — every problem, not just 5-6)
- CTA band: "Book a free audit"
- Footer

**Primary CTA:** "Book a free audit" → `/contact`

### 3. Work — `/work`
**Job:** Show build quality and range without exposing real client names/sites.
**Sections:**
- Nav

- CTA band
- Footer

**Primary CTA:** "Start your build" → `/contact`

### 4. About — `/about`
**Job:** Build trust in the solo-founder model before the call.
**Sections:**
- Nav
- About lead (expanded from Home version — founder story, no-handoffs pitch)
- How We Work (3-step band, reused)
- How We Build (4-phase panel, reused)

- Footer

**Primary CTA:** "Book a free audit" → `/contact`

---

### 5. Contact — `/contact`
**Job:** Capture the lead and set expectations for the call (no pricing pre-call).
**Sections:**
- Nav
- Intro line (what happens on the call, what doesn't — no quote given here)
- Contact form (name, email, one-line problem description, optional budget range)
- Direct contact: hello@vaelt.xyz, @vaeltlabs
- Footer

**Primary CTA:** Form submit → "Request sent"

---

## Hard Rules (apply site-wide)
- No price numbers anywhere on the site.
- No real client names, logos, or live demo links to actual delivered projects.
- Every page ends in a path to `/contact`.
- Copy never promises "customers" — use inquiries, leads, visibility, lead flow.
- One voice throughout: direct, plain, no agency jargon.
